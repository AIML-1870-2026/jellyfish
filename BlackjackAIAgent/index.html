Blackjack with AI Agents

This app uses a Claude or GPT model as an AI agent that reads the current game state and recommends the optimal blackjack action.

Explanation Levels: Basic — one sentence. Statistical — probabilities and EV direction. In-Depth — full analysis including bust probability, EV comparison, and card-counting considerations.

Risk Profiles: Conservative prefers standing in borderline cases and avoids unnecessary doubles. Balanced follows strict basic strategy. Aggressive doubles and splits more liberally to maximise EV.

Strategy Matrix: Shows the standard basic-strategy hard-total decision table. Your current situation is highlighted in gold.

Performance Analytics: Tracks win rate, P&L, streaks, and how often you followed the AI's advice.

Privacy: Your API key is parsed in-browser, held only in memory, and never stored or transmitted beyond the provider API.

Rules: Standard Las Vegas blackjack — dealer stands on soft 17, blackjack pays 3:2.
